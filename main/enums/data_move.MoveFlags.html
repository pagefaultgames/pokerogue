SOUND_BASED

SOUND_BASED: 8

Sound-based moves have the following effects:

  • Pokemon with the Soundproof Ability are unaffected by other Pokemon's sound-based moves.
  • Pokemon affected by Throat Chop cannot use sound-based moves for two turns.
  • Sound-based moves used by a Pokemon with Liquid Voice become Water-type moves.
  • Sound-based moves used by a Pokemon with Punk Rock are boosted by 30%. Pokemon with Punk Rock also take half damage from sound-based moves.
  • All sound-based moves (except Howl) can hit Pokemon behind an active Substitute.

cf https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Sound-based_move
